More tips for your trip

Be clear about the type of accommodation you are looking for.

They are opinions of people like you who travel to discover and enjoy a destination. You can find them in most online booking centers and in accommodation reviews on the Internet. When evaluating these comments, take into account the proximity of the publication date and the type of traveler who makes it. On many occasions, the establishments themselves respond to comments with very useful information, and you can often filter the reviews by country of origin, language, or even type of tourist.

Read other travelers' comments

As a summary, in Spain, you will find hotels, rural houses, unique accommodations, aparthotels, tourist apartments, hostels, hostels, and campsites. In addition, each type of accommodation is divided into categories according to their services and characteristics. Simply put, hotels are the most widespread option and a very comfortable stay for sightseeing; If you are looking for a special space, we recommend checking out unique accommodations such as Paradores or the typical regional ones in each area. The apartments and aparthotels have a kitchen. Meanwhile, guesthouses, hostels, and campsites are cheaper alternatives.

Check what your rate includes

The room price may include additional services to the stay. For example, check the included meal plan. The most common is the inclusion of breakfast or half board (breakfast and a meal), and it is less common to include full board or minibar service in the room. Also, ask about other services according to your needs, such as Internet connection or air conditioning in the room, pick-up or transfer service to the airport, availability of laundry, swimming pool or gym, possibility of parking...

If your pet accompanies you

Always check before completing the reservation if pets are accepted in the accommodation. Their admission is usually more frequent in rural accommodation, and there are more and more dog-friendly hotels. Normally, reservations are usually limited to a single animal per room, and it is usually easier to accept pets weighing less than 10 kilograms. Of course, always carry all your pet's documentation in order with you.
