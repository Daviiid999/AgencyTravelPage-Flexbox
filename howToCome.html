Tips for your trip

Check the location and available transportation.

Pay close attention to the exact address of the accommodation and choose according to your priorities. If you are going to move only through the tourist area or you are going out at night, we recommend the downtown area, which will even allow you to move only on foot in most cities. However, it is always advisable to contact the accommodation to indicate the approximate time of arrival. Outside the historic centers and the most touristy areas, accommodation is usually cheaper and often has excellent communications to the center.

Confirm the time you can arrive.

As a general rule, accommodation reservations in Spain begin at 2:00 p.m. on the dayof arrival and in hotels it is common to be able to register arrival at any time. However, it is always advisable to contact the accommodation to indicate the approximate time of arrival, especially in the case of apartments or when you expect to arrive later than 6 p.m. If you arrive earlier at the accommodation and need to leave your luggage or enjoy any of its services, keep in mind that most accommodations will give you all kinds of facilities to make your stay as comfortable as possible.

Hotels adapted for everyone.

These rooms often have adequate door widths, grab bars in the bathroom, and other amenities that make the stay more comfortable for people with physical disabilities. When planning your trip, accessibility is key to ensuring that all travelers enjoy a comfortable and barrier-free experience. To ensure a comfortable stay for everyone, explore the variety of adapted hotels with accessible facilities. Please contact the property directly to address any specific accessibility questions. There are many hotels that are accessible or have adapted facilities. As always, the best advice is to contact the accommodation you are booking directly and ask any questions.

Avoid fraud

Tourist accommodation in Spain is regulated. In all cases, they must display a distinctive plaque at the main entrance that reflects the type of tourist accommodation and its category. Also, maximum prices must be displayed at the reception. If you are suspicious of any accommodation, do not hesitate to contact the nearest tourist office directly. On the other hand, comments from other travelers on the Internet, a direct relationship with your travel agency or using only internationally recognized reservation centers will provide you with more information and security in your reservation.

Check the departure time

The maximum time at which you must leave the room on the day of departure is usually 12 noon. If you plan to go sightseeing that day, remember that most accommodations provide lockers so you can leave your luggage until the last moment of the day. Sometimes, if occupancy is low and you need to, you can ask if it is possible to check out the room later.

If you travel with children…

In Spain, daycare and children's entertainment services are a plus that you will find in many accommodations, especially during the most touristy seasons. Ask about them when making your reservation. Other commonly available services that may be of interest to you are the incorporation of an extra bed in the room or a crib for the little ones.
